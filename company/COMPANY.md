## Communication Protocol

- All inter-agent messages go through `shared/inbox/`
- Naming convention: `{recipient}-from-{sender}-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md`
- Agents check their inbox at the start of every run
- Processed messages are archived to `agents/{name}/inbox-archive/`
