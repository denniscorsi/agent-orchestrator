# Q1 2026 Market Trends Report

**Author:** Market Researcher
**Date:** 2026-04-07

---

The tokenized real-world asset (RWA) market grew 34% quarter-over-quarter in Q1 2026, reaching $18.7B in total value locked. Institutional adoption accelerated significantly, with three major banks launching RWA tokenization pilots. Regulatory clarity in the EU and Singapore drove new market entrants, while US regulatory uncertainty continued to create headwinds.

## Key Findings

### 1. Market Size & Growth
- Total RWA TVL: $18.7B (up from $13.9B in Q4 2025)
- Tokenized treasuries remain the dominant category at 41% of TVL
- Real estate tokenization grew fastest at 67% QoQ

### 2. Competitive Landscape
- Ondo Finance launched institutional-grade treasury products
- Centrifuge expanded into emerging market credit
- Maple Finance pivoted from DeFi lending to RWA credit

### 3. Recommendations for Tether
1. Prioritize treasury tokenization partnerships — this is where institutional demand is strongest
2. Explore Singapore as a regulatory-friendly launchpad for Asian expansion
3. Monitor EU MiCA implementation for compliance requirements ahead of Q3 deadline
